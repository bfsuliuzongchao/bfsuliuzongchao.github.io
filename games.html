The Legend of Zelda
《塞尔达传说 荒野之息》

Step into a world of discovery, exploration, and adventure in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. Travel across vast fields, through forests, and to mountain peaks as you discover what has become of the kingdom of Hyrule in this stunning Open-Air Adventure. Your journey is freer and more open than ever. Take your system anywhere, and adventure as Link any way you like.

Animal Crossing
《集合啦，动物森友会》

Escape to a deserted island and create your own paradise as you explore, create, and customize in the Animal Crossing. You can hunt down insects at the crack of dawn, decorate your paradise throughout the day, or enjoy sunset on the beach while fishing in the ocean. Each day on your island is a chance to check in and find new surprises all year round.

Pokémon Legends
《宝可梦传说：阿尔宙斯》

Your adventure takes place in the Hisui region. You are tasked with studying Pokémon to complete the region’s first Pokédex. To do this, you’ll need to catch wild Pokémon by sneaking up and throwing a well-aimed Poké Ball. You can also toss the Poké Ball containing your ally Pokémon near a wild Pokémon to seamlessly enter battle.

Mario Party Superstars
《马里奥派对超级巨星》

The Mario Party series is back with 5 classic boards from the Nintendo 64 Mario Party games. Race to get the most stars (and sabotage your opponents) on boards like the stellar Space Land or the spooky Horror Land! Party at your own pace by suspending a game and picking up right where you left off! The tides can turn quickly in Mario Party, so stay vigilant, partygoers.

Overcooked! All You Can Eat
《分手厨房》

In a local cooperative experience, players control a number of chefs in kitchens filled with various obstacles and hazards to rapidly prepare meals to specific orders under a time limit. Enjoy hundreds of levels of cooperative cooking chaos across increasingly perilous and obscure kitchens.

Digital Games or Physical Games?

实体卡带可以直接插在主机上方的卡槽中运行，默认不需要额外下载，游戏进度和数据会保存在机身储存中。机身仅支持同时插入一张实体卡带，想玩另外的卡带游戏想要手动替换卡带。
数字版游戏需要从eshop线上商城中下载，但eshop网速较慢。Switch内置储存空间仅32GB，如果你想要购买数字版游戏，请另外购买一张大容量的microSD卡。数字版游戏下载后可以直接在首页打开并快速切换游戏。

怎么买最划算？

能够买到实体卡带的热门游戏就买卡带，全新或者二手都可以，最好是海外版.如果游戏通关了，或者不想玩了，可以二手卖掉回血，性价比很高。目前买不到实体卡带的小众游戏，或者是折扣很大的数字版游戏也可以入手。
